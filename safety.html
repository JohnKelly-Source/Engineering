Safety Concerns and the Implications on the School Environment

  • This design will not require permanent alterations to the space because they are drop down ceilings.
  • Fiberglass is environmentally friendly.
  • A gridwork of metal channels suspended on wires from the overhead structure. Each cell is then filled with panels.
  • dropped ceiling is a pathway for HVAC airflow, acting as an air plenum for the hot air return.
    • this air movement can combine with the other material above the ceiling to create a fire hazard. Therefore the ceiling needs to be cleaned. breathing airborne dust containing fiberglass may irritate the skin, eyes, nose, and throat.
    • This will only be a issue for the person cutting the fiberglass in that case they can wear safety material. This can also be a issue if the fiberglass is made from cheap material.
